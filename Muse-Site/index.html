Riverian pirates is a 2D mobile strategy game where you, as a pirate captain, can build ships, recruit your own crews of animals, sail down rivers discovering territories and challenging enemies such as other pirate ships for various treasures.

In the past, the folk of Riveria lived in peace and joy, spending their time adventuring into endless river mazes.

 

Until one day it all came to an end!

 

The Kraken and his naval fleet from salty waters took over Riveria and forced their own law over the land.

 

Now, the native folks of Riveria are rising in rebellion against Kraken's dictatorship and so begins the adventures of Riverian Pirates.
